üìå Project Summary

This project evaluates the Conversion Effectiveness Performance of multiple campaigns using KPIs such as Conversions, CPA, CVR, ROAS, and Device-wise conversion behavior. The goal is to identify which campaigns deliver the best cost efficiency, which sources drive quality conversions, and how device behavior influences overall funnel success.

  • Google PMax Apparel delivers the highest CVR (8.2%) and **lowest CPA**, making it the strongest conversion driver.
  • Facebook Retargeting Cart generates the highest conversions but needs CPA optimization.
  • Tablet users convert the best (9.4% CVR), making them a high-value segment.

üí° Recommendations

  • Increase budget allocation toward Google PMax and High Intent Search due to strong CPA + CVR combination.
  • Optimize Facebook Retargeting by refreshing creatives and controlling frequency to reduce CPA.
  • Improve desktop landing page UX to lift lower conversion rate.
  • Run targeted tablet-focused offers to maximize high CVR potential.
